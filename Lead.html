About Me

Bongani Mtimkulu


Born and raised in Gauteng, Sebokeng. Bongani is a final-year Information Technology student at the North-West University. His lower grade studies started at Oliver Lodge Primary, Vanderbijlpark. He then continued his secondary studies at Hoerskoel Suiderlig High School in Vanderbijlpark, where he went on to lead as the President of the RCL in his final year.

Bold and outspoken, He belives communication is the most important skill to have in this information-dominant world. Interacting well with others comes natural to him. His leadership skills are driven by the desire to constantly find, with the help of others, logical solutions to problems. He is defined by resourcefulness, organization, and a commitment to learn.

Having been exposed to the functions and technicalities of programming languages such as Python, C++, C#, and Java in the past three years, I desire to discover the various ways in which practical problems are technically solved. Standardized systems such as HTML , CSS and JavaScript are languages I have the ability to maneuver, as I have used them to develop this specific website.

Creatively; I am also a huge fan of video editing, logo designing and branding using Adobe Photoshop and Filmora.
